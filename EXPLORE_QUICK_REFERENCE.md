## 🌦️ BEST TIME TO VISIT

### Dry Season (Most Recommended)
- **Months**: April-October
- **Weather**: Hot, humid, occasional rain
- **Pros**: Better for trekking, fewer mosquitoes
- **Cons**: More crowded, higher prices

### Rainy Season
- **Months**: November-March
- **Weather**: Very wet, muddy trails
- **Pros**: Fewer tourists, lush landscapes
- **Cons**: Difficult trekking, slippery paths

**⭐ Best Months**: April-May, September-October

## 📝 PRACTICAL INFO

### Currency Exchange
- **USD to IDR**: 1 USD ≈ 15,000-16,000 IDR
- Most places take cash only
- ATMs available in major towns
- 💳 No credit card? Bring cash!

### Getting There
1. **International**: Fly to Jakarta
2. **Domestic**: Jakarta → Balikpapan (1.5 hours)
3. **Local**: Balikpapan → Kutai Barat (by car/boat)

### Language
- 🗣️ English: Limited outside tourist areas
- 🗣️ Bahasa Indonesia: Common
- 🗣️ Local Dayak: Used in villages
- 📱 Translation app recommended

### Safety
- ✓ Generally safe for tourists
- ✓ Respect local customs
- ✓ Avoid peak heat (12-3pm)
- ✓ Use guide for jungle activities
- ✓ Stay with group
