## General Questions

### Q: What is Eunoiaverse?
**A:** Eunoiaverse is a comprehensive digital platform that combines a content creation system, an e-commerce marketplace, and a secure wallet for managing finances. It's designed to provide a seamless experience for creators, sellers, and users. The platform also features a detailed travel guide for Kutai Barat, Indonesia.

### Q: What are the main features of Eunoiaverse?
**A:** The core features include:
- **Content Management:** Tools for creating and managing digital content.
- **Marketplace:** A space to buy and sell products and services, with a focus on local crafts from Kutai Barat.
- **Wallet System:** A secure digital wallet for payments, transfers, and transaction history.
- **User Authentication:** A robust system for user registration, login, and profile management.
- **Explore Page:** An interactive travel guide for planning trips to Kutai Barat.

### Q: Is Eunoiaverse free to use?
**A:** Creating an account and browsing the platform is free. Transaction fees may apply for marketplace sales and certain wallet operations. Please refer to the Wallet & Payments section for more details.

## Account & Profile

### Q: How do I create an account?
**A:** You can create an account by navigating to the "Register" page and filling out the required information, including a username, email, and a secure password.

### Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
**A:** Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your email address, and a link to reset your password will be sent to you.

### Q: How can I change my profile information?
**A:** Once logged in, navigate to your "Profile" page. From there, you can edit your personal information, change your password, and manage your security and notification preferences.

## Wallet & Payments

### Q: How do I add funds to my wallet?
**A:** You can add funds (deposit) to your wallet via the "Wallet" page. We support deposits through bank transfers from several major banks. Follow the instructions on the "Deposit" tab.

### Q: Are my funds and transactions secure?
**A:** Yes. We prioritize security. User passwords are encrypted, and we use prepared statements for all database interactions to prevent SQL injection. The platform also supports Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for an extra layer of security.

### Q: What are the fees for wallet operations?
**A:** We aim to keep fees low and transparent. Here is a summary:
- **Deposits:** Adding funds to your wallet via bank transfer is **free**.
- **Internal Transfers:** Sending funds to another Eunoiaverse user is **free**.
- **External Transfers (Withdrawals):** A processing fee of **0.5%** of the transaction amount (with a minimum fee of **Rp 1,000**) applies when transferring funds from your wallet to an external bank account.

### Q: Are there fees for buying or selling on the Marketplace?
**A:**
- **For Buyers:** In addition to the product price, a small service fee (e.g., Rp 1,500) and a community support contribution (e.g., Rp 2,500) are added at checkout. All costs are clearly itemized before you confirm your payment.
- **For Sellers:** Listing a product is free. A small commission is taken from the final sale price to cover payment processing and platform maintenance.

## Marketplace

### Q: How do I sell an item on the marketplace?
**A:** On the "Shop" or "Marketplace" page, registered users can create their own brand/store and list products. You will need to provide product details like name, description, price, and images.

### Q: What payment methods are accepted for purchases?
**A:** The primary payment method is the integrated Eunoiaverse Wallet. You can use your wallet balance to make purchases instantly.

### Q: Can I track my orders?
**A:** Yes. The "Shop" page has a "My Orders" tab where you can view the status of all your purchases, from "Awaiting Shipment" to "Completed".

## Kutai Barat Explorer

### Q: What is the "Explore" page?
**A:** The "Explore" page is a rich, interactive travel guide dedicated to the Kutai Barat regency in East Kalimantan, Indonesia. It provides information on destinations, cultural events, accommodation, local cuisine, and travel planning tools.

### Q: Can I book tours or accommodation through the Explore page?
**A:** Yes, the platform facilitates booking for tour packages and accommodation. You can view details, check prices, and make bookings for various destinations and events directly through the app, with payments processed via your Eunoiaverse Wallet.

### Q: How is the travel information kept up-to-date?
**A:** The information is curated by our team and local partners in Kutai Barat. While we strive for accuracy, we recommend cross-referencing key details like event dates or opening hours with local sources, especially for sacred cultural events.
